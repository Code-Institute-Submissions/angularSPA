Services

External Window Cleaning

Glass and window sills cleaned as standard.

Internal window Cleaning

Glass and window sills as standard. (Secondary double glazing is actually 2 sets of windows and therefore priced accordingly). Window cleaners carry protecting shoe covers for use if required when cleaning the insides. *Note – for insides we request that window sills are cleared of ornaments etc in advance of us calling. Also that furniture is not blocking access to the windows. The window cleaners’ public liability does not cover damage to curtains inside the house. Therefore it is at the customer’s discretion if they wish for us to clean the windows with the curtains hanging. We are very careful and so far, in 15 years have never caused damage.

Conservatory Roofs

Usually requested as an annual service we remove dirt, moss and other debris.

Gutter Cleanrance

Blocked gutters can cause serious damage! Before calling out a professional roofer it can be worth checking for simple obstructions like leaves or birds nests. We have also found the odd tennis ball lodged in a downpipe! We can clear your gutters, a service those who require it will usually need about once a year. We are usually over half the price of most roofing companies! We do not undertake repair work but can advise you if you need to call in a professional roofer. For our gutter clearing service we only need the length of the guttering requiring cleaning and how many floors are in the building. We charge £2 - £2.50 per metre for gutter clearance up to a height of 25 feet from the ground. For gutters over 25 feet up we would arrange an appointment for an assessment and quotation. For a ‘gutter cleaning only’ service we have a minimum charge of £15. If we are there window cleaning the minimum charge is £10.
